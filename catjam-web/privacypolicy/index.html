Privacy Policy

Thank you for using Cat Jam Heart Rate ("the app").

For the app to function, we collect and store the following data:

  • Basic information about your Google account, including email address, unique ID, and name
  • Heart rate data from your WearOS device while you're using the app
  • Basic anonymized analytics about your usage of the app

If you have any questions or would like your data to be removed, pleae contact the maintainer at eric@yoonicode.com.
